Project Design


Project Overview

  1. Application Description and Purpose:
    This purpose of this application is to show off the accomplishments of two sporting dogs. It will contain photos and embedded videos of the dogs' major wins, as well as some other information pertaining to breed standards and the types of sports they participate in.
  2. Intended Users:
    The intended users of this website will be the owner of the two dogs, as well as people who want a comprehensive timeline of their wins and development.
  3. Overview of Content:
    The page will be split into three sections: general breed information, information about Dog #1, and information about Dog #2. Each subsection will contain additional information. Section 1 will contain information like breed history, standards, temperment, and strengths. Section 2 will cover the activities and history of Dog #1. Section 3 will cover the activies and history of Dog #3.
