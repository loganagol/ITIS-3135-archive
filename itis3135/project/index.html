Meet Jubilee and Newt

Jubilee is a 6 year old German Wirehaired Pointer. She enjoys participating in a variety of dog sports, including obedience, rally, hunt tests, dock diving, lure coursing and barn hunt.

She is a witty girl who in her free time enjoys using skills she has learned to amuse herself. She frequently greets guests with a shoe, or a choice piece of dirty laundry lovingly plucked from the hamper. She is a sassy dog with a lot of opinions and doesn't hesitate to make herself heard.

Newt is a 1 1/2 year old German Wirehaired Pointer. He's still a newbie to the dog sports game but he's already dipped his paw into quite a few different games and earned a number of introductory titles. He has competed (or soon will) in conformation, obedience, rally, agility, hunt tests, field trials, dock diving, lure coursing, and barn hunt.

He is a sweet, cuddly boy with a heart of gold. He's a bit of a wimp, but what coddled mama's boy isn't? He shows a lot of promise and has a bright future ahead of him as a dog sport competitor.
